Manual QA checklist

  • Hover each marquee to confirm motion pauses and resumes.
  • Resize the browser and verify the loop stays seamless.
  • Tab into the page to exercise pause-on-focus.
  • Toggle the buttons above to validate the imperative API.
  • Check a reduced-motion environment to confirm animation suppression.
